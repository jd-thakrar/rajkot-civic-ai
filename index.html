Platform capabilities

Built for citizens. Powered for officers.

From WhatsApp-style reporting to GIS ward intelligence — everything Rajkot needs in one modern civic stack.

Multilingual by design

Submit in Gujarati, Hindi, or English via text, voice, photo, or WhatsApp-style chat. AI translates and classifies automatically.

Official RMC GIS wards

Real ward boundaries from gis.rmc.gov.in with 1,695 registered localities. Google Maps geocoding pins complaints to the correct ward.

Instant ticket IDs

Every grievance receives an official-format RMC ticket number. Citizens get confirmation; officers get structured, searchable data.

Ward analytics

Category breakdowns, urgency heatmaps, zone summaries, and priority-ranked civic works — all updated in real time.

Voice & photo intake

Speak your complaint or upload a photo. The system transcribes, detects the issue type, and assigns the ward without forms.

Proposal drafting

Officers can generate formal municipal scheme proposals from ranked projects — ready for Commissioner review.

Simple flow

From complaint to action in four steps

1

Report

Citizen submits via text, voice, photo, or chat in any supported language.

2

Classify

AI detects category, urgency, sentiment, and translates to English for officers.

3

Route

Area registry + geocoding assigns the correct RMC ward and ticket ID.

4

Act

Dashboard prioritizes works, maps hotspots, and drafts proposals for approval.
